Where can I see orders that are pending and complete?

The 6 most recent completed orders and the 6 nearest upcoming orders can be seen in your Dashboard and All Other orders can be found in the orders page.

How do I add new orders to my roster?

To add new orders your roster, you must complete the following steps:

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard page or to the Orders page, and click the "New Order" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Fill out the required feilds in the form.
  3. Click "Submit"
  4. Review your new order
  5. Click "Save"

Your new order should appear in both your Dashboard and Orders pages.
