Where can I see orders that are pending and complete?

The 6 most recent completed orders and the 6 nearest upcoming orders can be seen in your Dashboard and All Other orders can be found in the orders page.

How do I add new orders to my roster?

To add new orders your roster, you must complete the following steps:

  1. Navigate to your Dashboard page or to the Orders page, and click the "New Order" button on the top right corner of the page.
  2. Fill out the required feilds in the form.
  3. Click "Submit"
  4. Review your new order
  5. Click "Save"

Your new order should appear in both your Dashboard and Orders pages.

Can i update any orders that are already saved in the Orders page?

  • You can update any order that has not been paid yet. If you need to add items to the order, it is recommended to add a New Ordering Ticket using the Customer's information. If you need to remove sold items to a paid order, please fill out the form found here and someone from our Customer Satisfaction Team will contact you as soon as possible.
  • Note: If you update an order that has been marked as "Complete", the order will automatically revert to "Pending".

Can I Delete Orders?

  • Any order that has not been paid, can be deleted. If you need to delete a paid order, please fill out the form found here and someone from our Customer Satisfaction Team will contact you as soon as possible.
  • Note: If you delete an order that has been marked as "Complete", the items in the order will be considered as waste which may affect your food cost variance.
